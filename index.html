Profile

Donovan Frazier

I'm a 22-year-old web developer, writer, historian, video editor, and game developer. I graduated from the University of California, Riverside with a Bachelor degree in History and a Bachelor degree in Creative Writing.

I primarily studied world history and the literary world, but I also have a background in computer science and psychology.

My experience includes video editing, designing online tutorials, coding web pages such as this one (inspect my pages to check out my code), hosting public speaking events, and more. In my spare time, I write manuscripts in speculative fiction, develop video games, and sketch.
