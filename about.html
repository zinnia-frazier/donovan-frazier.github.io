My Story

Still in the early stages of my career, I've made accomplishments and learned under two companies, Innovetive Petcare and LifeHikes, as a marketing intern. Because of my experiences, I was inspired to pursue digital marketing as a profession and enrolled in a bootcamp hosted by UT Austin, where I'm currently working on various projects related to digital and content strategy.

At UC Riverside, I studied film and served as the Vice President of the game development organization at University of California, Riverside. This experience gifted me the ability to lead a community of gaming enthusiasts, host writing and career-readiness workshops, and support the future generation of game developers.
