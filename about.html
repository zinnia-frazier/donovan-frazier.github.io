About

More about me

I'm a 22-year-old web developer, video editor, writer, historian, and game developer. While studying at the University of California, Riverside, I worked on educational online tutorials for the Department of Teaching and Learning

I graduated from the University of California, Riverside with a Bachelor degrees in History and Creative Writing. I primarily studied world history and the literary world, but I also have experience in computer science thanks to working on projects in E-Learning and with the game development club, Gamespawn.

For school and work, I have done video projects, designed web pages, organized and hosted speaking events and used a variety of tools such as Camtasia, GitHub, DaVinci Resolve, Articulate 360, and HTML.

In my spare time, I write manuscripts in speculative fiction, develop video games, learn programming, and sketch.
