More about me

I'm a 22-year-old writer, historian, web developer, and game developer. I graduated from the University of California, Riverside with a Bachelor degrees in History and Creative Writing.

I primarily studied world history and the literary world, but I also have a background in computer science and psychology.

In my spare time, I write manuscripts in speculative fiction, develop video games, and sketch.
