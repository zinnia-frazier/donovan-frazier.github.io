Code Ninjas Video Commission

Video Commission - Video Editor

A video project commissioned by Code Ninjas for their event titled "Girls in STEM." This video project utilized film from another videographer, cutting that film based on the instructions of the commissioner, and layering text and effects on top of the film.
The video can be found on YouTube.

  • Cut video clips into a collage of clips based on the theme of the section
  • Balanced the audio of the speakers to each other as well as the background music
  • Applied video and audio transitions to enhance smoothness of speaker clips
  • Utilized a combination multiple effects, A-roll, and B-roll footage
  • Adjusted project timeline based on updates from the commissioner with regard to the demographic information of the audience expected for the event
