Digital Learning Objects

Online Tutorial LMS - Designer

Online tutorials covering a variety of topics in STEM and Humanities. Creation of these tutorials required learning E-Learning software such as Articulate 360, using various html elements where relevant. Due to these tutorials covering a wide range of topics, this required learning the fundamentals of the subject being taught.
Available in the UC, Riverside Library GitHub.

  • Storyboard, outline, and design online tutorials for courses of 300 or more students
  • Reach a total of 10,000 undergraduate students through the deployment of online tutorials and educational videos
  • Embed the online tutorials in LMS portals such as Canvas and Blackboard
  • Work independently as well as with a team of teaching librarians to design the tutorials
  • Some of the tutorials published have been awarded by PRIMO's Site of the Month award

Interested in an example?

Below are images of a before and after iframe embedded survey for a tutorial titled "Get it at UC." This tutorial teaches students how to use University of California database feature. The survey asks students how knowledgable they are of the Get it at UC button. The image on the left is a net promoter survey of how knowledgable 147 students are of the Get it at UC button before beginning the tutorial.

After completing the tutorial, students were asked how helpful the tutorial was in how to use the Get it at UC button. The results are on the left image.

The surveys demonstrated that the tutorial was effective at informing students about core features of the database the University of California use through hands on learning.
