Tech Talks

What are Tech Talks?

Tech Talks are speaking events hosted by the game development club, Gamespawn. Industry professionals have the opportunity to present for about 15 minutes. The next 45 minutes are an interview / Q/A.

Types of Speakers

AAA and Indie Studios
