Video Commission - Video Editor

A video project commissioned by Code Ninjas for their event titled "Girls in STEM."

  • Balance the audio of the speakers and the background music
  • Apply transitions and audio levels to enhance smoothness of speaker clips
  • Utilize a combination multiple effects, A-roll, and B-roll footage
  • Adjust project timeline based on updates from the commissioner about the audience of the event
