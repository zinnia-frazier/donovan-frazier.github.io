WWII's Impact on Vietnam and the Philippines

Video Essay - Videographer

A research video essay deailing with the complex question of how two particular countries in Southeast Asia were affected by WWII in a compare and contrast analysis.
The full video can be found on YouTube

  • Conduct original research, script, film, and edit a video essay for a final project for a history
    of Southeast Asia upper division class
  • Utilize a combination of historical photography, film, and documentation to establish A roll
    and B roll footage
  • Record a voice over and interlace the cuts of the audio with the transitions of the film
  • The entire project was completed within a 3 week period
